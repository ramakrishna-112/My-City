Visit Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is a vibrant city known for its rich history, culture, and modern growth. Founded in 1591 by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah, the city is famous for its iconic Charminar, historic monuments, and old markets filled with traditional crafts and delicious food. Today, Hyderabad is also a major technology hub, often called “Cyberabad”, with IT parks, start-ups, and global tech companies. The city is well-known for its world-famous Hyderabadi Biryani, warm hospitality, and the unique blend of old and new architecture. Whether it's history, food, or innovation, Hyderabad offers something special for everyone.

Top three activties to do in Hyderabad

Explore Hyderabad’s iconic monument, the Charminar, located in the heart of the Old City.Walk through Laad Bazaar, famous for bangles, pearls, and traditional shops. The area gives a real feel of the city’s culture and history.

Enjoy a peaceful lakeside experience at Hussain Sagar. You can take a boat ride to the giant Buddha statue in the middle of the lake or stroll around Lumbini Park, especially beautiful at night.

A must-visit attraction, Ramoji Film City is one of the world’s largest film studios. It offers guided tours, live shows, film sets, gardens, and activities for all age groups — a full-day entertainment experience.
